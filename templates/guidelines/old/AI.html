7. Learning Path & Roadmap (Click to expand)
Beginner (0-6 Months) (Click to expand)
  • ✅ Learn Python, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib
  • ✅ Study Probability, Statistics, Linear Algebra
  • ✅ Understand Supervised & Unsupervised Learning
  • ✅ Work on simple ML projects
Intermediate (6-12 Months) (Click to expand)
  • ✅ Master Deep Learning with TensorFlow/PyTorch
  • ✅ Work on NLP & Computer Vision Projects
  • ✅ Deploy ML Models using Flask, FastAPI
  • ✅ Learn about MLOps tools
Advanced (12+ Months) (Click to expand)
  • ✅ Study Advanced Topics (Transformers, GANs, RL)
  • ✅ Work on Large-Scale AI Models
  • ✅ Contribute to Open-Source AI Projects
  • ✅ Research AI Ethics & Responsible AI
